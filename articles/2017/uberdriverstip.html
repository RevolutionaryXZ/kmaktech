Uber drivers make $50M in month 1 of tipping

  Uber has recently decided that it will go through many changes. They accounced this plan about 2 months ago, and we are already seeing the outcome. Uber calls the plan "180 Days of Change."

  One of the most notable changes that took place, was the adding of the tipping feature. Which (clearly) allows Uber riders to send their driver a tip, based on the distance of the ride. Since the adding of tips, drivers have racked up a total of $50 Million USD, in the first month.

  Another change in the application was altering the window of time the rider is able to cancel a ride, from 5 minutes to 2.

  The campaign came about a week after Uber fired some of their executives and when their CEO resigned. Uber is still waiting for a new CEO to fill the gap.
